KaamConnect collects user details, contact information, location permission, booking details and payment status only for providing local service marketplace features. Contact details remain hidden until booking acceptance and payment verification.

Users must provide correct information, use the platform only for legal work, avoid fraud, harassment or unsafe activity. Admin may block fake or unsafe accounts.
