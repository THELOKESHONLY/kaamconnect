Policies

Privacy, Terms & Data Deletion

Clear policies help global users trust your platform.

Privacy Policy

KaamConnect does not sell user data. Phone numbers and addresses are used only for booking and service completion.

Location is used only to show nearby verified workers and can be removed from the Data Control section.

Terms & Conditions

Customers must post real work. Workers must provide correct skill, city and profile details.

Unsafe behaviour, fake profiles and fraud can lead to account blocking.

Data Deletion

Users can request account deletion from the Data Control section.

Admin reviews deletion requests and removes user data according to platform safety needs.

Country Rules

Country, language and currency can be changed from the top selector.

For real global launch, country-specific tax, worker and payment rules should be added later.
