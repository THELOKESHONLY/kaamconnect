Questions

Frequently asked questions

How does RapideService work?

Customers post work, workers send bids, customers accept a bid, payment is verified, contact unlocks and review is given after completion.

Is worker contact visible immediately?

No. Customer contact and full address are protected until worker is accepted and payment verification is completed.

Can workers join for different skills?

Yes. Workers can register under skills like electrician, plumber, cleaner, driver, tutor, mobile repair, freelancer and more.

Is mobile app available?

The website is mobile-friendly. Android and iOS apps can be launched after website testing and domain setup.
